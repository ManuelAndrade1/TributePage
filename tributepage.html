Viktor Frankl

The man who survived Auschwitz


Here's some information about him:

  • 1905 - Born in May 26th.
  • 1921 - First Public Lecture.
  • 1926 - Logotherapy.
  • 1937 - Becomes a doctor.
  • 1942 - Sent to Auschwitz.
  • 1945 - Liberated in April 23rd.
  • 1946 - Publishes Man's Search for meaning.
  • 1997 - Dies.
